About NILIN Air

VISION

To normalize regional air mobility

MISSION

To develop an eVTOL ecosystem

NiliN Air provides urban and suburban air transportation that is quick, efficient, and environmentally sustainable. Our E-VTOLs, which are autonomous UAVs, transport passengers and goods between drone ports quickly and safely, avoiding traffic and shortening travel times.
